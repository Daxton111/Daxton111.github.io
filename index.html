Introduction: Iceland is the perfect family trip for those in need of relaxation mixed with adventure. Iceland is one of the most beautiful places to visit and I'm confident that you won't regret going.


    Icelandic Attractions:
  • Cuisine
  • Sights
  • History

Food: Travel to Iceland to try there wonderful variety of foods. Such as, fish, lamb, rye bread, and many more. So try not to miss out on the tasty foods that will make your mouth water.

Views: There are many exciting places to visit in Iceland that will leave you amazed. For example, go to the blue lagoon to let off some steam, or the famous Vatnajökull, where you can go searching for glaciers.

Culture: Iceland has a lot of viking heritage, which also relates to there unique Old Norse language. These were all very interesting to learn about, when checking out the Art Museum Reykjavik.
